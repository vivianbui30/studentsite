Cocoon Original

Website Redesign

  • CLIENT: College
  • PROJECT TYPE: Website Redesign
  • COMPETENCIES: UX/UI Design
  • SOFTWARE: Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma, Visual Studio Code

It's not about ideas. It's about making ideas happen.

The Challenge

For this project, my goal was to introduce a user-friendly, visually appealing website while promoting Vietnamese cosmetic products and promoting sustainability, utilizing eco-friendly and advocating for recycling initiatives.

The Solution

I decided to go for a minimal design with an earthy tone color palette while maintaining the brand philosophy: raw, organic materials; vegan; green lifestyle.

SITEMAP

STYLE GUIDES

For the typography, I decided to go with an organic serif typefaces, and paired with a clean and minimal sans-serif for the body text to increase readability.

Style A: I want to focus on the founders' nature love. I chose this color palette which easily reminds people of nature, giving people a sense of calmness and relaxing.

Style B: I decided to keep the original color which is derived from their own logo, an earthy yellow-toned color palette.
