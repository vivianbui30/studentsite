Design is thinking made visual.

The Challenge

My task for this project was to design magazine layouts by using given images and content, while following typography rules, grid systems, and white space.

The Solution

I decided to focus mostly on pairing typefaces, balancing white space, and establishing text hierarchy, and also incorporating images with content.
